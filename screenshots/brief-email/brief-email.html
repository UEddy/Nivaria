New competitor brief

Acme Analytics HIGH THREAT

What changed

Acme launched a usage-based pricing tier undercutting your mid-market plan

Executive summary

Acme replaced its flat $99 Team plan with a usage-based tier starting at $39, and now advertises a 14-day trial with no card required. The pricing page reframes their value around per-seat flexibility aimed squarely at growing teams.

Recommended response

Lead with total cost of ownership at scale and your predictable flat pricing. Prepare a side-by-side for deals where the buyer is comparing per-seat math against a fixed annual number.

Key talking points

• Their entry price looks lower, but usage-based billing gets unpredictable past 20 seats.
• Highlight your included onboarding and the fact that there is no metered overage.
• Trial-to-paid friction: ask where their no-card trial leaves the buyer after 14 days.
